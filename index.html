About Us

AN TOP LINE FACILITIES MANAGEMENT SERVICES LLC is a prominent company in the field of steel and aluminium fabrication and technical works, renowned for its extensive expertise in structural steel and stainless steel fabrication, as well as its proficiency in electromechanical and civil works for industrial, residential, and commercial buildings.

Who We Are

AN TOP LINE FACILITIES MANAGEMENT SERVICES LLC

The Company was established in 2010 and, situated in Al Quoz Industrial Area1- Dubai. The establishment encompasses 5000 sq. of covered space and is outfitted with top-quality tools and cutting-edge technologies.
