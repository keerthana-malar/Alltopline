About Us

AN TOPLINE FACILITIES MANAGEMENT SERVICES LLC is a prominent company in the field of steel and aluminium fabrication and technical works, renowned for its extensive expertise in structural steel and stainless-steel fabrication, as well as its proficiency in electromechanical and civil works for industrial, residential, and commercial buildings.

Who We Are

AN TOPLINE FACILITIES MANAGEMENT SERVICES LLC

The Company was established in 2010 and is situated in Al Quoz Industrial Area 1- Dubai. The establishment encompasses 2500 sqft. of covered space and is outfitted with top quality tools and cutting-edge technologies. We have a team of professionally qualified and highly experienced engineers and technicians who are dedicated to delivering the best quality results on time.
